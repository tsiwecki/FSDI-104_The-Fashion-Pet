About Us

The Fashion Pet is proud to serve our local communities which includes Pontiac, Streator, Ottawa, Peru, LaSalle,and Morris, Illinois.


We are the leading pet groomer in the area serving our communities. We offer dog, cat, bird as well as specialty pet grooming.


Our grooming services include full service pet grooming, bathes, teeth, nail trimming and grinding, coloring, and much more. For all your pet grooming needs, please contact us so we can serve you.
